PU, PVC & PE BELT

We have a wide variety of thermoplastic belts made from PU (Polyurethane), PVC (Polyvinylchloride) & PE (Poly Ethylene) to solve your light to medium duty conveyor belting needs for the various material-handling requirement, from package handling to raw material processing applications including special surfaces for package handling, grease and oil resistant belts, non-marking, and static conductive belts, also EU / FDA approved and tested for food contact materials.

We offer also endless belt, which cut and jointed according to your needs and exact dimensions provided by the customer. If required due to the installation complexity, we offer also services to performed jointing at customer’s company premises.
